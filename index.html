Training Pathway Forward

  • Center of mass positioning: Landing mechanics training

Medium-term Goals (Weeks 5-12)
  • Reactive strength development: Plyometric progressions
  • Elastic energy utilization: Bounce and rebound training
  • Sprint transition mechanics: Acceleration to max speed
  • Performance monitoring: Regular reassessment

Despite current limitations, Jude demonstrates solid foundational fitness and power potential (75th percentile standing bound). The identification of a single primary deficit provides a clear, focused training pathway with high probability of significant improvement.

Expected Outcomes: With dedicated quadriceps strengthening and technical refinement, improvements of 0.3-0.5 seconds in 0-10m times and elimination of the reactive strength deficit are realistic targets within 12-16 weeks.
